CEO & Founder of beryl.cc

Emily is Founder + CEO of Beryl (formerly Blaze) and the inventor of the Laserlight. She is dedicated to developing solutions for safe urban cycling, with technology and design at the core. The journey began as a university project in 2012, launched on Kickstarter in 2013, and now ships to over 60 countries worldwide. After Laserlights were fitted to the London Bike Share scheme, Emily was honoured with an MBE from the Queen, for her entrepreneurial endeavours and 'services to transport and the economy'. This is Emily's third time Creating on Kickstarter, and she's incredibly excited to return to the platform where the first Laserlight concept was launched, over six years ago.
